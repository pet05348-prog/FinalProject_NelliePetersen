Introduction

I am a first year student attending University of Minnesota, Crookston. I am an online student going for a degree in Information Management. I am a returning student to college. I attended college right after high school in 2000, but my major then was English: Creative Writing and due to failing a class and it only being offered once every 4 semesters, I ended up not completing my degree then. I then later, in 2016, attended a technical college to become a Pharmacy Technician. Due to health issues, I had to leave that field and am now pursuing a degree that would be easier to do remotely.

About Me

I have always been an avid reader and since I was quite young, I had always dreamed of writing a book. I had started writing several different things, but never got very far. A few years ago while I was still working as a Pharmacy Tech, I had started a book and got about 6 or 7 chapters in but put it down and never finished it.

Another time I started freewriting and ended up writing a warning message from an AI system to its user. I put that freewriting aside but after my health issues started, I found it again and wrote 2 short novels based on the warning and self-published them. The idea was those 2 books were the start of a much longer series, but I ended up getting distracted and haven't written more in the series yet. I still intend to go back to the books, but as of now, I am focusing on schoolwork.

While working on novels, I started to use AI image generators to make images of my characters and some scenes I envisioned. I had also generated images over random thoughts I had and novels I had read, so I decided to post the images I created.

Mostly due to disinterest in them, but also somewhat due to my health issues, I am pretty much a homebody. Besides writing and reading books, I enjoy reading manga and binging tv shows. I mostly watch fantasy or supernatural shows, but I also enjoy medical and crime dramas, musicals and action movies with a lot of explosions. I despise reality tv shows though. If I am going to be watching scripted drama anyway, I might as well watch people who are acting well...
