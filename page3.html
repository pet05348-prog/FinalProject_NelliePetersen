From Novels I read:

As I was reading different novels, I got interested in creating images based on characters from those novels.

Ragdoll girl

This prompt was based on the main character of a fan-translated Chinese webnovel that I read called Rebirth in the Interstellar Meow Meow Meow. The main character is a little girl who can change in a ragdoll cat but also has a in-between state with ears and tail on her human body.

Prompt 14: ragdoll cat as a little 5 year old girl with white hair and really blue eyes

King Kong Barbie

In yet another Chinese martial arts novel called Complete Martial Arts Attributes set in modern times, the main character attends a international martial arts competition and one of his opponents is described as "King Kong Barbie" because, while she is attractive, she is very tall and muscular and I wanted to see what kind of image that label would create.

Landscapes/images from novels I read

These are images generated from descriptions in novels that I read that seemed interesting.

Prompt 20: a blood red eye peering out of a dimensional rift in the sky surrounded by black clouds and red and purple lightning

In the webnovel Complete Martial Arts Attributes, I found a description of a "demon god" attempting to pierce through dimensions and I really wanted to see an eye peering out of black clouds and surrounded by lightning...

Style: Anime

Style: Magick

Style: Mystefiction

Prompt 21: a lake covered in white pupae and thousands of large dragonflies flying above it

In another Chinese webnovel called Global Game: AFK In The Zombie Apocalypse Game, the main character is forced to live part of his life in a deadly game where he gets a cheat that allows him to summon clones as zombies and gets more and more as he levels up. As he was exploring another world in the game, he came across a lake covered in pupae from oversized deadly dragonflies and I just thought that would be a cool image.

Style: Magick

Style: Semi Realistic Anime
