Alternate Versions Of Known Characters from Comics or Anime

It has been a while since I have been obsessed with comics and anime, but there were a few years when I couldn't get enough of them. I have favorite characters in both Marvel and DC. Most of the anime I watched was Japanese dubbed and only occassionally subbed.

Wolverine

Wolverine was always my favorite Marvel character. Of all the teams in Marvel, the X-men were my favorite. The major issue with that was all the interconnectedness of the individual and team titles though. In both Marvel and DC, they would have storylines that occurred through several team titles and several individual titles. I was a little obsessed with knowing the full story, so I would often feel like I had to read the beginning of a title up to the issue that connected to the multi-issue storyline and it just got to be too much.
