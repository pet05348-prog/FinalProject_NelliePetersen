Images from a Sleeping Novel I Wrote, But Never finished

This is a novel that I started but didn't finish. The premise of the novel was similar to another webnovel I had recently finished reading, called Shadow Slave, in that both novels explored the idea of nightmares taking form and humans who ended up fighting monsters in this nightmare world could eventually use the same powers in real life. As I was writing it, I took the descriptions of characters I created and tried using them as prompts. I did a few prompts related to buildings I mentioned and monsters I was thinking of introducing later.
