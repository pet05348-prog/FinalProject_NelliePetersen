Images Inspired by a Novel I Wrote and Self-published Called Everyone Has a System Now

I started writing a novel a few years ago and while I stopped working on it for a while and then went on to finish it, in the middle of working on writing the book I was playing around with image generators and decided to use an image generated from starryai.com to put on the cover for my self-published book. I had a great deal of trouble getting an image I like with silver hair, red eyes, and no face on the t-shirt. There was also the thought that some of the images could be placed in the book as illustrations, but I never found any I liked enough to do that.

Feel free to submit your views on all, none or just some of the images! Click on the image to open a larger image in a new window. Highlighted parts of prompt are what I considered the major points, but you are free to judge from your own perspective.

FYI, I don't really consider the prompt very successful if it put a face on the t-shirt...

Alex

The main character of the novel is Alex. He woke up one morning to discover he had a System that would allow him to do quests, fight monsters, and level up. While he was messing around with the System, he discovered that the System allowed him to change his appearance and he ended up getting stuck with silver hair and red eyes. These images were the result of me attempting to get an image of him after he changed his appearance.

Tyke

One of the things the main character got in the first book was a new puppy he found lost and while I haven't finished it yet, I did start a book from the dog's perspective and these prompts were my attempt to secure an image for the eventual cover. Of all the prompts I have done, I do think these images were the ones that came out closest to what I was imagining.

Red Cord

In a scene in the book, the main character activates a skill that will lead him to the portal the hostile alien is coming from and I pictured it as red spots of light floating from all over the place and converging to form a red cord that leads him to the portal. Wormhole/Portal

For the second novel in the series, a hostile alien arrived on Earth through a wormhole/portal. These prompts were attempts to get an image for that cover. My goal was to get an image of a portal opening on the lawn in front of a normal looking house, maybe with an alien in front of it, but I had no luck with that at all and ended up having to go with the best image of a wormhole on a white background.
